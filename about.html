About Rashmika

The journey of a musical artist bridging Nepal and India through melody

The Voice Connecting Cultures

Rashmika Luitel is a Nepali-Indian singer and songwriter known for her soulful voice and cross-border musical collaborations. Since her debut in 2019, she has performed across 15+ cities in Nepal and India, blending traditional rock with contemporary sounds.

100+ live performances since 2019
12 original songs released
Performed in 8 Indian states and 3 Nepali provinces
Collaborated with 20+ artists across genres

Her music often explores themes of cultural identity, love, and social issues, resonating with young audiences across South Asia. Rashmika works closely with WQ Studio and BnT Musiz to produce music that transcends borders.

When not performing, she mentors young artists and participates in cultural exchange programs between Nepal and India.
